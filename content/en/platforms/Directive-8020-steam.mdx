## PC Performance Prep for Directive 8020 on Steam

Even before final system requirements are posted, you can make smart readiness choices. Narrative horror games often depend on stable frame pacing, good lighting performance, and clean audio playback. That means “it launches” is not enough—you want a smooth, uninterrupted first run.

### Pre-launch technical checklist

| Area | Minimum Good Practice | Better Practice for Launch Week |
|---|---|---|
| **Storage** | Keep **50+ GB free** on SSD | Keep **80+ GB free** for patches and cache |
| **GPU Driver** | Update monthly | Install newest stable game-ready driver pre-launch |
| **CPU/RAM** | Close background-heavy apps | Create a clean startup profile |
| **Audio** | Verify headset/surround settings | Test spatial audio profile for horror immersion |
| **Display** | Confirm refresh rate and HDR | Build per-game calibration preset |

### Graphics priorities for cinematic horror titles

For likely gameplay style in Directive 8020, prioritize settings in this order:

1. **Frame stability** over max effects  
2. **Shadows/volumetrics** at balanced levels  
3. **Texture quality** based on VRAM headroom  
4. **Reflections and post-processing** tuned last  
5. **Motion blur/grain** based on personal comfort  

If your build is mid-range, start from a medium-high profile and test in dark scenes first. Horror pacing is more sensitive to stutter than many competitive genres.

> **Warning:** Don’t benchmark only bright scenes. In many horror games, low-light sequences stress different settings and can reveal stutter that normal test areas miss.

## Smart Launch-Day Plan for Directive 8020 Steam Buyers

Launch day can be chaotic: queue delays, first-hour patches, and social spoilers. You can avoid most pain points with a simple operations plan.

### Launch-day action plan

1. **Pre-load immediately** if available.
2. **Apply driver update 24 hours before launch**, not at launch minute.
3. **Disable nonessential overlays** (capture, chat, RGB services) for first session.
4. **Verify file integrity** if you see crashes before changing many settings.
5. **Play first hour spoiler-free** before checking forums.
6. **Back up save path** after first stable session.

| Launch Risk | Early Symptom | Fast Fix |
|---|---|---|
| **Shader stutter** | Hitches during first scenes | Let shaders complete, restart once |
| **Audio desync** | Dialogue drift in cutscenes | Recheck output device + sample rate |
| **Unexpected fps drops** | Major dips in dark corridors | Lower shadows/volumetrics first |
| **Crash on startup** | Black screen or instant close | Verify files, update VC/runtime packages |
