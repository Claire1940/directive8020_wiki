## The "Bad" Endings: Total Extinction and Infection

While most players strive for survival, the darker conclusions of Directive 8020 offer some of the most visually stunning and narratively impactful scenes in the franchise. The "Total Extinction" ending occurs when the player fails the final confrontation on the bridge, leading to the ship's self-destruction.

However, the most insidious of the **Directive 8020 all endings** is the "Infection" ending. If a character is infected but manages to hide their symptoms, they will successfully board the escape shuttle. The final cutscene reveals the entity reaching Earth's orbit, effectively ending the human race.

> **💡 Tip:** If you are aiming for the "All Die" trophy, focus on making impulsive, aggressive choices and intentionally failing the "Keep Calm" heartbeat segments.

## FAQ

**Q: How many total endings are in Directive 8020?**

A: While there are 4 main narrative "states" (All Survive, All Die, Partial Survival, and Infection), there are over 12 unique cinematic variations based on which specific characters make it to the escape pod and the state of their relationships.

**Q: Can I save everyone if I fail a QTE?**

A: In some cases, yes. Directive 8020 features a "Backup Plan" system where another character can sometimes intervene to save a failing teammate, but this usually requires that the two characters have a "Close" relationship status.

**Q: Does the difficulty setting affect Directive 8020 all endings?**

A: The difficulty affects the window for QTEs and the aggressiveness of the AI during stealth segments. While the story remains the same, "Lethal" difficulty makes it significantly harder to achieve the "All Survivors" ending due to tighter reaction times.

**Q: Is there a secret ending after the credits?**

A: Yes. If you collect all 10 "Secret Memories" throughout the game, an additional scene plays after the credits featuring the Curator, teasing the next installment in The Dark Pictures Anthology Season 2.
