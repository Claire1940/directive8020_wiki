## Who is Brianna Young?

Brianna Young is the protagonist of *Directive 8020*, serving as a highly skilled pilot aboard the colony ship *Cassiopeia*. Unlike previous protagonists in the series who often find themselves in horror scenarios by accident, Young is a professional operating in a high-stakes environment. However, professional training can only do so much when faced with an extraterrestrial threat that defies biological logic.

Lynch describes Brianna as a "cat" who always attempts to land on her feet, regardless of the chaos surrounding her. This adaptability is her greatest strength but also a source of internal conflict. She is someone who aspires to greatness but often questions her own instincts due to the overprotective nature of her mentors.
