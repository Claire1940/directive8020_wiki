Unlike the purely cinematic entries of the past, this game incorporates real-time stealth and combat mechanics. 1.  **Use the Scanner Frequently:** Your handheld scanner can detect biological anomalies. Use it before entering new rooms to identify mimics.
2.  **Hold Your Breath:** The "Don't Breathe" mechanic returns but is now integrated into real-time hiding spots. Watch the UI closely to maintain composure.
3.  **Environmental Distractions:** Use the ship's systems to create noise in distant rooms, drawing threats away from your path.
4.  **Combat as a Last Resort:** Engaging in direct combat is the fastest way to lose a character. Always look for an escape route or a stealthy workaround first.

## Managing the Mimic Threat

The core horror element of the game is mimicry. The alien organism can take the shape of your crewmates. This creates a "social deduction" layer to the survival gameplay. If you accidentally help a mimic instead of a real crew member, you are directly contributing to the death of another astronaut.

To successfully execute **Directive 8020 how to save everyone**, you must use the "Verification Protocols" found throughout the ship. These are limited-use items or stations that can confirm a character's identity. Saving these for high-tension moments where the crew is split is the most effective way to prevent an internal massacre.
