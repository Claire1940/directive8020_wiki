## Safe Buying Workflow for a Directive 8020 Steam Key

This is the practical checklist most buyers skip—and later regret. Follow these steps in order to reduce payment, activation, and account issues.

### Step-by-step purchase checklist

1. **Start with official data**
   - Confirm game title, publisher, and release timing.
2. **Shortlist trusted sellers**
   - Official Steam + known authorized key stores.
3. **Validate listing text**
   - Platform, edition, region, and bonus terms.
4. **Compare total price**
   - Include tax, fees, and payment surcharges.
5. **Check delivery timing**
   - “Instant delivery” is not guaranteed for preorders.
6. **Archive proof**
   - Save invoice + listing screenshot.
7. **Activate immediately when received**
   - Don’t wait beyond return windows.

| Risk Point | Common Mistake | Safer Move |
|---|---|---|
| **Region mismatch** | Ignoring region label | Buy only global or local-region key |
| **Wrong edition** | Assuming Deluxe is included | Verify exact edition name |
| **Late delivery** | Buying from unknown seller at launch | Prefer stores with clear fulfillment SLA |
| **No refund option** | Skipping policy page | Read preorder/cancel terms before paying |

### Activation steps on Steam

Once your key arrives:

1. Open Steam client.
2. Go to **Games** → **Activate a Product on Steam**.
3. Enter the code exactly as provided.
4. Confirm product name before final acceptance.
5. Install and verify library entry.

If activation fails:

- Re-check typing and hidden spaces.
- Confirm region eligibility.
- Contact seller support with screenshot and timestamp.
- Escalate through payment provider if unresolved.
