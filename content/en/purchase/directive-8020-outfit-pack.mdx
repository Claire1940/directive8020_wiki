1. **Set a default loadout first**  
   Keep a lore-friendly baseline so premium looks feel meaningful when swapped in.

3. **Switch at chapter breaks**  
   Avoid frequent mid-scene changes; it can weaken continuity.

4. **Save one “finale” style**  
   Holding back your favorite look adds impact late in the campaign.

5. **Capture screenshots intentionally**  
   If you create content, choose outfits before key cinematic segments.
