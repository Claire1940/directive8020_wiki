## Platform and Region Checklist Before You Buy

Physical buyers still run into preventable issues in 2026: wrong region codes, incompatible DLC entitlement, and language/packaging mismatch. Treat this section as your final pre-check before payment.

### Compatibility checklist table

| Checkpoint | What to Verify | Pass Condition |
|---|---|---|
| **Platform SKU** | PS5 vs Xbox version labeling | **Exact console match** |
| **Region code** | ESRB/PEGI/CERO-style print | **Matches your account/store region** |
| **Language support** | Box text + in-game language note | **Preferred language confirmed** |
| **DLC redemption region** | Voucher terms if included | **Redeemable in your account region** |
| **Internet requirement** | Install/patch note on listing | **You can patch on launch day** |

### Region-risk quick guide

| Scenario | Risk | Recommendation |
|---|---|---|
| **Import copy + local account** | Medium | Verify DLC and language support first |
| **Domestic copy + domestic account** | Low | Safest for day-one play |
| **Third-party marketplace listing** | Medium to High | Buy only with detailed photos and SKU proof |

If your goal is the cleanest **Directive 8020 physical release** experience, domestic retail is the least friction path. Imports can be great for collectors, but they demand extra verification.

> **Warning:** “Region free” for disc boot-up does not always mean “region free” for bonus content redemption.
