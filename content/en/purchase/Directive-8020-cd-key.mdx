1. Log into the Steam account you plan to use permanently.
2. Open **Games** in the top menu.
3. Select **Activate a Product on Steam**.
4. Enter your **Directive 8020 cd key** exactly as provided.
5. Confirm the license and complete activation.
6. Check your library for Directive 8020 and preload/download when available.

### Common activation errors and fixes

- **“Invalid Product Code”**: Recheck characters like O/0 and I/1.
- **“Region Restricted”**: Your account region does not match the key.
- **“Already Used”**: Contact seller support with purchase proof immediately.
- **Game not appearing in library**: Restart Steam client and verify account login.

If support is needed, open a ticket quickly and attach:

- Screenshot of key
- Error message capture
- Account region confirmation

Fast, complete proof usually improves resolution speed.
